End-to-End Hiring Flow

The complete lifecycle of a job requisition.

1

Job Created

A Company posts a new job requirement outlining skills, budget, and location.

2

Recruiters Assigned

The job is delegated to a Vendor or directly assigned to independent Recruiters.

3

Candidates Sourced

Recruiters gather resumes or Candidates apply directly via the portal.

4

AI Matching

Resumes are parsed, analyzed, and scored against the JD for optimal alignment.

5

Submission

The best matches are shortlisted and officially submitted to the Company.

6

Hiring

The Company interviews candidates and marks the requisition as filled upon success.
