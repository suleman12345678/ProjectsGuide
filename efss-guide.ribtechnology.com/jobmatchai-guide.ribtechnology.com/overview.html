JobMatch AI – Job Management System

AI-powered recruitment platform for managing the complete hiring lifecycle.

Description

JobMatch AI is a centralized recruitment platform designed to streamline hiring across companies, vendors, recruiters, and candidates. It leverages artificial intelligence to automate resume analysis, candidate matching, and job workflows, enabling faster and more accurate hiring decisions.

System Roles & Architecture

Admin

Full control over platform users, companies, and system operations.

Company

Post jobs, manage applicants, and track hiring performance.

Vendor

Manage recruiters and client hiring workflows seamlessly.

Recruiter

Source candidates and manage submissions for multiple clients.

Candidate

Search jobs, apply with AI matching, and manage professional profile.

Core AI Features

AI Resume Parsing

Automatically extract candidate details, skills, and experience from any document format with high precision.

Smart JD Matching

Intelligent scoring engine that matches candidates against job descriptions using advanced semantic algorithms.

Bulk Resume Analysis

Process and analyze hundreds of resumes in minutes, dramatically reducing time-to-hire for high-volume roles.

Semantic Smart Search

Discover the perfect talent using natural language queries that understand intent beyond simple keywords.

Key Use Cases

  • End-to-end recruitment lifecycle management
  • External vendor and agency collaboration
  • Automated candidate screening and shortlisting
  • Centralized talent pool and application tracking
  • Data-driven hiring with advanced analytics
  • Seamless candidate experience and profile management
