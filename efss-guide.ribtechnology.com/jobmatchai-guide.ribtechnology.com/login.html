Authentication & Access

Secure entry points for all platform roles.

JobMatch AI provides a unified authentication system that securely manages access across all platform roles. Users log in through a single portal, and based on their role, they are automatically redirected to their respective dashboards.

Unified Login Portal

The platform uses a centralized authentication gateway with role-based routing. Once authenticated, users are directed to their specific workspace such as Admin, Company, Vendor, Recruiter, or Candidate portals.

  • Role-based access control (RBAC)
  • Secure session management
  • Automatic role-based redirection

Unified login interface for all platform users.

Role-Based Access Routing

After successful login, users are automatically routed to their designated portal based on their assigned role.

  • Admin → /admin
  • Company → /company
  • Vendor → /vendor
  • Recruiter → /recruiter
  • Candidate → /candidate
