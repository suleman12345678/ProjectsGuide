Dashboard Documentation

The central intelligence hub of JobMatch AI, designed to provide real-time visibility into the entire recruitment ecosystem.

Overview

The JobMatch AI Dashboard serves as the primary operational gateway for all platform roles. It is meticulously engineered to synthesize complex recruitment data into actionable insights, allowing users to monitor performance, identify bottlenecks, and execute critical tasks without navigating through deep sub-menus.

Whether you are an Admin overseeing global metrics or a Recruiter managing a specific pipeline, the dashboard dynamically adapts to your role, surfacing the most relevant information and "Quick Actions" to maximize daily productivity.

What You Can See

The top section of the dashboard features high-level metric cards that provide an instantaneous pulse check of your recruitment activity:

  • Total Jobs: Cumulative count of all job requisitions ever created.
  • Active Jobs: Positions currently open and accepting applications.
  • Applications: Total volume of candidate submissions received.
  • Shortlisted: Top candidates ready for the interview stage.

Why It Matters

In a high-velocity hiring environment, the dashboard acts as a force multiplier for recruitment teams:

Faster Decision Making

AI-driven prioritization ensures you focus on the highest-potential candidates and most urgent jobs first.

Visibility into Hiring Pipeline

Eliminate data silos by providing a single source of truth for all hiring stakeholders across the organization.

Operational Efficiency

Reduce time-to-hire by automating the collection of metrics and providing direct paths to common workflows.
